Presentation skills

Improve the impact of your event.

The UC Davis "ASK ME" framework is designed for extension, but also can help you develop your talk.

  • Audience and their interests
  • Solution - the information that will meet their interests and needs
  • Key message - the key points they need to hear and remember
  • Message form and delivery - the best way to engage the audience and communicate your message
  • Evaluation - collecting feedback to assess success and improve
