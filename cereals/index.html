Cereals

CIMMYT and IRRI are working with National partners to address the primary problems of

  • Maize (Water scarcity, Low nutritive value, and poor seed quality), and
  • Rice (Environmental stresses, postharvest losses). 
  • Wheat (Rust, Insufficient pasta, residue management and late wheat planting),
