Database of Existing Videos (from Dr Diane Barret, UC Davis)

Dr. Barrett reviewed numerous existing videos available on fruit and vegetable postharvest handling, preservation and quality evaluation and made a database which is summarized in this document: Fruit and Vegetable Videos. (It will open in Microsoft Excel). Below is a list of video titles in alphabetical order for each subject area, with a link to each video.

All of the videos are produced by government or public institutions except for the ones titled "Commercially Produced".
