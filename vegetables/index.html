Vegetables

One of the major challenges and opportunities facing vegetable production is overall profitability. The associated opportunities are: 1) Improved seed and imporved varieties (tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, chili, bitter gourd, marrow), 2) Integrated and improved insect and disease management, and 3) Vegetable Production Under Protective Structures
