Citrus True-to-Type

True-to-type plants are genetically identical to their female parent. True-to-type trees lead to greater consistency in fruit quality and quantity, which improves product marketability. Improving true-to-type trees starts in the tree nurseries. Improvements will involve scion, rootstock and nursery tree management.
